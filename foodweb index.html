About

Food Web is an E-canteen inspired by Food Ordering System.
It allows you to pre-order your lunch and snacks
without going to the school canteen.
Accessible, Free, and Convenient

Statement of the Problem

1. Skipping breakfast resulting to hunger during class hours.
2. Crowded canteens during lunchtime.
3. No time to buy lunch or snack because of overloaded schedule.

Concept & Idea

Our System called Food Web is inspired by Food Ordering System
Wherein it allows customers to place an order at virtually any time, from anywhere, saving the time and resources typically spent on travelling to pick up a meal.

Pros

1. Convenience
- Enables your students to place orders while they have tight schedules.
2. Pre-Order
- It's grab & go after you've placed your pre-order.
3. Simple & Stress-Free
- Won't wait in line anymore especially when canteens are over crowded during lunch time.
4. Prevents Food Insecurity
- College students today are having trouble eating healthy due to a lack of time and funds.

Cons & Loss of Data

1. Power Outage and Connectivity
- The system is online-based basically it needs internet connection.
2. Loss of Data
- We made sure that any information or data that we input in the system is being kept on the database.

Start

Our system comes with a log-in interface.
So, in order to access the Food Web menu, you need to have an account for you to log in. When starting the system, there will be three options that will have to choose:
1. Sign Up
2. Log In
3. Exit

Sign Up

Option 1

If you choose option 1 in the system you will be directed to sign up page wherein you will be asked to fill up the following details:
Full name, Username, Password, Confirm Password
The data inputted in the system will be saved into the database.
Then after signing up, Food Web menu will show up wherein you are able to choose a variety of dishes to order.

Log In

Option 2

If you choose option 2 in the system you will be directed to log in page wherein ou will be asked to fill up the following details:
Your Username and Password
Any information or data that you inputted when you signed up in the system is being kept on the database
So, when you log in the database will read your saved data on the database. After you log in, Food Web menu will show up wherein you are able to choose a variety of dishes to order.

Exit

Option 3

If you choose option 3 in the system which is "Exit"
"Thank you, please come back again" will be displayed on the screen and the system will return to 0
